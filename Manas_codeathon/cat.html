ABOUT GAME

In this game you choose your avatar which is your pet. Please note that you should choose your pet whose characteristics represent you the most. After your selection, your character is born, meaning your pet's level is 0. Your pet grows as level increases..

How to Increase level?

Your are provided with activites or daily tasks which helps you to understand and improve your mindfulness. At the completion of each task you are given a token.
TOKEN - is like a daily nutrition which helps your pet to grow.
Example:
for a Dog : you get a bone token,
for a Cat : you get a fish token ,
for bunny : you get a carrot..
After collecting 5 tokens your level is increased by 1.
Now you may notice that your pet has grown. This shows that gradually you are more mindful.

About...

Cats are known for their independent nature, and people who choose cats may appreciate this quality. Cats often seek out quiet and peaceful spots for rest and relaxation. Cat owners often understand and respect their cats' need for personal space and boundaries, which may reflect their own respect for personal boundaries. Cats are generally good at taking care of themselves when it comes to grooming and using the litter box. This can attract people who appreciate self-reliance in their pets
